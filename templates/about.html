About CIT MSc Programs

Coimbatore Institute of Technology offers a range of five-year integrated MSc programs, providing students with an immersive and comprehensive learning experience in various fields of study.


MSc Programs Offered:

  • M.Sc. Software Systems
  • M.Sc. Data Science
  • M.Sc. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • M.Sc. Decision and Computing Sciences

Programme Details:

  • Programme Duration: 5 Years
  • Programme Fee: Rs. 73,600/- for the first semester
  • Admission Fee: Rs. 4,200/- (once at the time of admission)
  • Sanctioned Strength for each Programme: 60

Eligibility Criteria:

Applicants must have passed the HSC/equivalent exam with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as subjects of study. However, candidates with an aggregate of 75% and above in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry or Computer Science are eligible to apply.
