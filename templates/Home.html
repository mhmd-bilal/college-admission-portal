Admission Process

1

Read the instructions

Please read the instructions carefully before proceeding. These guidelines are designed to ensure a smooth and efficient application process for all candidates. Failure to adhere to the instructions may result in delays or rejection of your application.

3

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria by entering your academic marks. This step allows us to evaluate your qualifications and determine your eligibility for admission to Coimbatore Institute of Technology's prestigious MSc program.

2

Submission of Personal and Academic Details

Once the student meets the eligibility criteria, it is of utmost importance that they meticulously furnish their personal details, as well as their 10th and 12th-grade marks, ensuring accuracy throughout the process.

4

Payment Verification and Follow-Up

After completing the details, the student is required to proceed with the payment. Upon successful payment, an email will be sent to the student outlining the subsequent steps in the admission process.
